A videogame with many classic worldwide board games

All board games

1
Connect Four

In Connect Four, players take turns dropping colored discs into a vertical grid. The objective is to be the first to form a line of four of your own discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

2
Mancala

In Mancala, players strategically move seeds around the board, aiming to capture the most seeds by the end of the game, with each turn involving distributing seeds from one pit counterclockwise and capturing seeds if the last one lands in an empty pit on the player's side.

3
Gomoku

In Gomoku, players take turns placing stones on a grid, aiming to create an unbroken row of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, with strategic placement and careful observation being key to victory.

4
Checkers

In Checkers, players strategically move their pieces diagonally across the board, aiming to capture their opponent's pieces by jumping over them, with the goal of either eliminating all opponent's pieces or blocking their movement entirely.

5
Dominoes

In Dominoes, players take turns placing tiles with matching numbers or suits, aiming to empty their hand while strategically blocking opponents, with the winner being the first to play all their tiles or achieve the lowest score when no more moves are possible.

6
Durak

In Durak, players strategically play cards to attack and defend against their opponents, aiming to be the first to get rid of all their cards or avoid becoming the 'durak' (loser), with each round presenting tactical challenges and opportunities for deception.

7
Chess

In Chess, players maneuver their pieces across the board, strategically capturing their opponent's pieces and aiming to checkmate their opponent's king, with each piece having specific movements and abilities, leading to endless possibilities and strategic depth.

8
Chinese Checkers

In Chinese Checkers, players aim to move their pieces from one side of the hexagonal board to the opposite side, strategically hopping over other pieces and creating chains, with the goal of being the first to move all their pieces to the opposing triangle.

9
Ludo

In Ludo, players race their tokens around the board, strategically navigating obstacles and opponents' tokens while aiming to reach the center home square, with each move determined by the roll of a die and opportunities to knock opponents' tokens back to the starting area.

10
Backgammon

In Backgammon, players strategically move their checkers around the board, aiming to bear them off while blocking their opponent's progress, with each roll of the dice presenting tactical decisions and opportunities for advancement.
